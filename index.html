What is UR prints?

UR prints is a website held by one college student (me), here you will be able to buy pre-made models or custom models. I plan to add more customisation (like color or light effects) to my prints in the future

Since this website is in no way professionnal your only method of payment is Paypal

How to buy a print:

When you click buy you will be have to first certify that you're not a bot (sorry I know it's anoying but without it the website would probably explode), you will then have to give me contact informations so that I can notify you on the progress of your print, the prices for delivering the print will have to be discussed by mail since the website doesnt support this feature right now (but hopefully will in the future). When the price for the print(s) has been established I will start the printing(if needed, I sometimes have prints already in stock), after that you will be notified of the progress of every steps of the print, from printing to shiping (shiping informations may differ depending on the company delivering the package). If you wich to buy more than one print at once just fill the form for the two different prints with the same contact information and I will merge them myself. If you need any other informations you can send me a message in the contact pange above.
